First Aid Guide

Simple steps while help is on the way

🩸
Bleeding
Press cloth firmly on wound. Do not remove.
🔥
Burns
Cool with clean water for 10 minutes.
🦴
Fracture
Do NOT move the limb. Keep still.
😵
Fainting
Lay flat, lift legs above heart level.
💓
Heart Attack
Sit down, loosen clothing, stay calm.
🌡️
Choking
Lean forward, 5 firm back blows.
